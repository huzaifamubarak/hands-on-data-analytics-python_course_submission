## How to Submit!!!

### One-Time Setup

You only need to do this once for the whole course.

1. Complete your assignments in your own assignment repository (created from the course template).
2. Push your solutions to GitHub.

### Weekly Submission Steps

1. **Fork this repository** - Click the Fork button in the top-right corner.

2. **Navigate to the correct week's folder** in your forked copy:
   - `submissions/week1/`
   - `submissions/week2/`
   - etc.

3. **Create your submission file:**
   - Click **"Add file"** ---> **"Create new file"**
   - Name it exactly as your GitHub username: `YOUR_USERNAME.md`
   - Add the following content:

   ```markdown
   # Your Full Name
   - **GitHub Username:** @YOUR_USERNAME
   - **Assignment Repository:** https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/da-python-assignments
   - **Week:** 1
   - **Comments (optional):** Any notes you'd like to share.
   ```

4. **Commit the file** directly to the `main` branch of your fork.

5. **Open a Pull Request:**
   - Click **"Contribute"** ---> **"Open pull request"**
   - Ensure the base repository is `Muhammad-Lukman/hands-on-data-analytics-python_course_submission` and the base branch is `main`
   - Title your PR: **"Week X Submission - Your Full Name"**
   - Click **"Create pull request"**

6. **Done!** Your submission will be reviewed and merged. Once merged, your entry will appear in the submissions table above.

## Submission Status

- ✅ **Merged** — Your submission has been received and is complete.
- 🔄 **Under Review** — Your pull request is open and awaiting review.
- ❌ **Changes Requested** — The instructor has requested modifications. Check your PR comments.

---

## Important Notes

- Your assignment repository must be **public** to be reviewed.
- Submit one pull request per week, even if you update your assignment repo later — the link will always point to your latest work.
- Do not modify other students' submission files in your pull request.
